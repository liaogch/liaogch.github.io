Research Interests

I mainly focus on data privacy, with emphasis on

  • differential privacy, privacy-preserving machine learning
  • game theorectical analysis, economic mechanism desgin
  • federated learning
I am interested in developing novel mathematical models in optimization, game thoery, and economics to study real-world systems such as social networks, crowd-learning systems, and Internet of Things.

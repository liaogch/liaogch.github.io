Research Interests

  • Data privacy: differential privacy, privacy-preserving machine learning
  • Crowd computing: federated learning, edge computing, Internet of Things
  • Network economics: network optimization, game theory, mechanism design
In general, I am interested in developing novel mathematical models in optimization, learning, and game theory to study networked systems such as edge computing systems, crowd-intelligent systems, and Internet of Things.
